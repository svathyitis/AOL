About Spanda Karika

Spanda (स्पन्द) means the divine throb or pulse — the vibration underlying all of creation. Karika means verses. Written by Vasugupta's disciple Kallata (~9th century CE), Kashmir.

  • One of the foundational texts of Kashmir Shaivism
  • 25 sutras in 2 sections — Shuddha Spanda & Sahaja Vidyodaya
  • Explores: consciousness, maya, liberation, divine grace
  • Gurudev's commentary is considered rare & precious

"The universe is a play of consciousness — pulsating, alive, aware." — from the tradition

Bangalore Ashram

Art of Living International Centre

21st Km, Kanakapura Road · 250+ acres on Panchagiri Hills

A world unto itself

Established 1986. Home of Gurudev. Headquarters of the Art of Living Foundation — the world's largest volunteer-based NGO.

  • 250+ acres of serene hilltop campus
  • Visitors from 182+ countries every year
  • Visiting hours: 9:00 AM – 9:00 PM
  • 28 km southwest of Bengaluru city
🏔️ Panchagiri Hills · Kanakapura Road · Bengaluru

Vishalakshi Mantap

The iconic 5-tiered meditation hall at the heart of the ashram — a stunning blend of modern and Vedic architecture. Open for group sadhana and meditation every day.

  • Daily group sadhana each morning
  • Silence zone — phones off, no loud talking
  • Guided meditations at 12 PM & 4 PM daily

A Day at the Ashram

  • 🌅 5:00 – 6:30 AM — Morning sadhana (Padmasadhana, Pranayam, Bhastrika, Kriya)
  • 🍽️ 7:30 AM — Breakfast · Annapurna Kitchen
  • 🎥 11:30 AM & 2:00 PM — AOL video shows · Info Centre
  • 🧘 12:00 PM & 4:00 PM — Guided meditation · Info Centre
  • 🌸 Tue & Thu ~4 PM — Darshan with Gurudev
  • 🎵 6:30 – 9:00 PM — Satsang (Sun: 4:30–6:00 PM)
  • 🔱 Monday — Rudra Puja · Yagnashala
📅 Daily rhythm · Subject to announcements at Satsang

Evening Satsang 🎶

The soul of ashram life — music, bhajans, meditation and wisdom every evening. Open to all.

  • Mon–Sat: 6:30 PM – 9:00 PM
  • Sunday: 4:30 PM – 6:00 PM
  • Vishalakshi Mantap or Yagnashala
  • Live music, chanting, guided meditation
  • Followed by Spanda Karika talks by Gurudev
  • Free & open to all visitors

"Satsang — where scattered minds unite through music, meditation, and wisdom."

🎵 Every evening · Free entry · All are welcome

🍛 Annapurna Kitchen

One of the largest solar-powered kitchens in the world — producing up to 40,000 sattvic meals per day.

  • 100% vegetarian · no eggs, meat or fish
  • Powered by solar panels
  • Serves ashram residents, programs & visitors
  • All meals prepared as prasad (sacred offering)
  • Volunteers can do kitchen seva — a beloved tradition
  • Organic produce from ashram farms

Drinking water available at taps marked "Drinking Water" throughout campus.

🌿 Sattvic · Solar-powered · Seva in every meal

🐄 Goshala — Cow Sanctuary

A living sanctuary for indigenous Indian cow breeds, integral to ashram life and Vedic tradition.

  • 100+ indigenous cows of rare breeds
  • Agnihotra fire ritual daily at sunrise & sunset
  • Mahamrityunjaya Homa performed daily
  • Cows milked twice daily — milk distributed in ashram
  • Cow seva open to visitors (brushing, feeding)
  • Ghee & cow dung used in homas, made in-house
  • Organic farming uses goshala compost
🔥 Agnihotra at sunrise & sunset · Goshala seva open to all

🔱 Shiva Temple & Yagnashala

Sacred spaces woven into the daily rhythm of ashram life.

  • Shiva temple on campus — open daily for darshan
  • Rudra Puja every Monday at Yagnashala
  • Veda Agama Samskrutha Maha Patashala on campus
  • Agama Pathshala: 5-year training in puja, homa, Vedic chanting
  • Veda Pathshala: Vedic education & Sanskrit
  • Daily homas performed by trained students
  • Vedic ceremonies: marriages, thread ceremonies, more
🕉️ Vedic Education · Traditional Puja · Yagnashala

📚 Sri Sri Gurukul & Schools

Education rooted in both ancient wisdom and modern knowledge.

  • Sri Sri Gurukul spread over 7 acres on campus
  • Agama & Veda pathshalas for traditional learning
  • 2,000+ free Sri Sri Ravi Shankar Vidya Mandirs across 22 states
  • Education for tribal and underprivileged children
  • Computer literacy, modern + Vedic curriculum
  • Music, yoga & meditation integrated into learning
🎓 Ancient wisdom · Modern skills · Free education

🏥 Sri Sri Ayurveda Hospital

A multi-specialty Ayurvedic hospital on campus — integrating traditional healing with scientific research.

  • Full Panchakarma & Ayurveda treatments
  • Built in traditional Kerala architecture style
  • Conducts scientific research on Ayurvedic medicines
  • Treatments for stress, pain, chronic conditions
  • Doctors trained in both Ayurveda & modern medicine
  • Open to ashram guests and program participants
🌿 Panchakarma · Research · Traditional Kerala Style
